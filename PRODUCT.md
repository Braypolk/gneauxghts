# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Gneauxghts is for people who want Markdown notes without the overhead of complex organization systems. They prefer capturing thoughts quickly, keeping notes as plain files, and letting context, search, links, tasks, and semantic retrieval bring the right notes back when they are needed.

## Product Purpose

Gneauxghts is a local-first desktop notes app that keeps writing, task capture, and note retrieval focused and low-friction. Notes remain regular Markdown files, tasks emerge from Markdown checkboxes, and semantic search works quietly in the background so users can trust the app with their notes and ideas without managing a heavy workspace.

## Brand Personality

quiet, fast, interconnected

The product should feel personal, focused, and trustworthy. AI-assisted behavior should read as useful background magic, not as advertised buzzwords or a design centerpiece.

## Anti-references

Do not make Gneauxghts feel like Notion's broad do-everything workspace. It should not feel like enterprise software, a dashboard suite, or an AI-forward product that constantly markets its intelligence. Avoid visual complexity that makes the app feel design-led instead of notes-led.

## Design Principles

- Keep the note at the center; surrounding UI should support writing and retrieval without competing for attention.
- Make organization emergent; users should not need to maintain elaborate structures before the app becomes useful.
- Let intelligence stay quiet; semantic and AI features should improve flow without becoming the product's visible identity.
- Preserve local trust; plain Markdown files, system conventions, and predictable behavior should reinforce user ownership.
- Optimize for keyboard-first speed; common note, search, task, and navigation flows should feel immediate.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Respect the user's system theme and reduced-motion preferences. Keep keyboard-first workflows strong, visible focus states reliable, and text contrast suitable for sustained reading and note editing.
